Frequently Asked Questions

Is Delta Executor safe to use?

Yes, Delta Executor is completely safe when downloaded from our official website. We do not include any malware, viruses, or unwanted software. However, be cautious of fake versions from unofficial sources, which may contain harmful software.

Why do I need to complete the key system?

The key system helps us maintain our servers and continue providing Delta Executor for free. It also helps prevent abuse and ensures only legitimate users can access our service. The process only takes a minute and the key is valid for 24 hours.

How often is Delta Executor updated?

We update Delta Executor regularly, typically within hours after Roblox updates. This ensures our executor remains undetected and compatible with the latest version of Roblox. You can check our Discord server for the most up-to-date information.
